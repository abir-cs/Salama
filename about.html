About Us

Salama Clinic

Salama Clinic is a modern medical center offering a calm, reliable space for patients and families. With a focus on comfort and professionalism, the clinic provides eight key services, covering both general care and specialized treatments.

Clinic History

Founded a few years ago by a small group of dedicated healthcare professionals, Salama Clinic began with a simple goal: to offer accessible, trustworthy care to the community. Since then, it has grown steadily, expanding its team and services while keeping the same patient-focused approach.

Values and Philosophy

We are guided by values of respect, clarity, and quality. We believe in honest communication, attentive care, and maintaining high medical standards. Our philosophy is centered on supporting the overall well-being of every patient across our eight specialties, including General Medicine, Cardiology, Pediatrics, Dermatology, Gynecology & Obstetrics, Emergency Care, Medical Imaging, and Laboratory Tests.
