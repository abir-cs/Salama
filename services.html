Our Services

General Medicine
+
Our general medicine department provides comprehensive care for adults of all ages. Our physicians focus on preventive health, early diagnosis, and long-term management of chronic diseases.

Services include:

  • Routine immunizations and wellness checks
  • Diagnosis and management of conditions like diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and respiratory infections
  • Vaccinations and preventive health advice
  • Coordination of specialist referrals for complex health issues

Why choose us:

Our general medicine team ensures your overall health is monitored, helping you prevent complications and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Cardiology
+
Our cardiology department is dedicated to the health of your heart and vascular system. We combine advanced diagnostic tools with expert care for prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart-related conditions.

Services include:

  • Diagnosis and treatment of coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, heart failure, and high blood pressure
  • Non-invasive procedures: ECG, echocardiography, stress tests
  • Interventional procedures: cardiac catheterization, angioplasty (if needed)
  • Heart health consultations, risk assessments, and lifestyle counseling

Why choose us:

Our cardiologists provide early detection and treatment of heart conditions, working closely with patients to optimize heart health.

Pediatrics
+
Our pediatric services provide compassionate care for infants, children, and adolescents. We focus on growth, development, and prevention of illness.

Services include:

  • Routine immunizations and wellness checks
  • Diagnosis and treatment of common childhood illnesses and chronic conditions
  • Growth and developmental monitoring
  • Nutritional guidance, behavioral counseling, and parental support

Why choose us:

Our pediatric team ensures your child receives age-appropriate care in a friendly and safe environment.

Dermatology
+
Our dermatology department treats a wide range of skin, hair, and nail conditions while also providing cosmetic solutions.

Services include:

  • Treatment for acne, eczema, psoriasis, fungal infections, rashes, and skin allergies
  • Skin cancer screenings and mole evaluations
  • Hair and nail disorder treatments
  • Cosmetic dermatology: anti-aging treatments, pigmentation correction, laser therapy

Why choose us:

Healthy skin is essential for confidence and overall well-being. Our dermatologists combine medical expertise with advanced treatments for optimal results.

Gynecology & Obstetrics
+
We offer comprehensive women's healthcare across all stages of life. Our team provides both preventive care and specialized treatment.

Services include:

  • Routine gynecological exams and Pap smears
  • Prenatal care, childbirth, and postnatal support
  • Fertility counseling and family planning
  • Management of menstrual disorders, menopause, and reproductive health conditions

Why choose us:

Our compassionate care ensures women receive personalized attention for reproductive health, pregnancy, and overall wellness.

Emergency Care
+
Our emergency department provides rapid, 24/7 care for life-threatening conditions and urgent medical needs.

Services include:

  • Immediate treatment for trauma, accidents, fractures, burns, and severe infections
  • Cardiac and stroke emergencies
  • Stabilization and transfer to specialized care if necessary
  • Advanced resuscitation and life support

Why choose us:

Quick, efficient emergency care can save lives. Our team is trained to respond to any medical crisis with expertise and speed.

Medical Imaging
+
Our imaging services use advanced technology to diagnose and monitor medical conditions safely and accurately.

Services include:

  • X-ray, CT scan, MRI, ultrasound, and mammography
  • Imaging for cardiovascular, neurological, orthopedic, and abdominal conditions
  • Image-guided procedures and interventions
  • Accurate interpretation by experienced radiologists

Why choose us:

Early and precise diagnosis helps doctors develop effective treatment plans, ensuring better patient outcomes.

Laboratory Tests
+
Our laboratory services provide fast and reliable testing to support diagnosis and monitor treatment progress.

Services include:

  • Blood tests: complete blood count, glucose, cholesterol, liver, and kidney function
  • Urine and stool analysis
  • Infectious disease testing (COVID-19, flu, hepatitis, etc.)
  • Specialized tests: hormonal, genetic, and allergy tests

Why choose us:

Our modern lab ensures accurate results with quick turnaround times, helping patients and doctors make informed health decisions.
